The problem

Groundwater in most parts of the world is contaminated, often containing arsenic or heavy metals.

Arsenicosis, or chronic arsenic poisoning through consumption, can cause a wide variety of symptoms and illnesses, including skin and lung cancer, heart disease, gangrene, and diabetes, among others.

About 300 million people in the world are exposed to elevated levels of arsenic in drinking water.

The World Health Organization (WHO) enhanced concentration limit for As in drinking water is 10μg/L. However, countries such as Argentina, Chile, United States, Mexico, Bangladesh and China failed to reach this value (~40μg/L).

Our products

Smart filters for the purification of water thanks to nanotechnological membranes, to filtratie arsenic, toxic agents, heavy metals, viruses and bacteria.

Our filters with special nanotechnological membranes can filter contaminants and even remove arsenic. The membrane system is connected to the source from which the water is taken to make it drinkable. Our nanotechnological membranes can filter Chlorine, Arsenic, Viruses, Bacteria, Spores and Heavy Metals.
